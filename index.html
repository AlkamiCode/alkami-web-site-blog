What Do You Do?

So what does your company really do?

We create elegant mobile friendly apps for the web. Every app we build is responsive across all screen sizes, and conforms with conventional best practices. Besides just programming, we love to design and create memorable content. We believe making an app is like building a custom high performance vehicle minus the expensive maintenance costs of course. We work with newly started businesses as well as established companies that need to offload some of their work. We provide you with well documented, well factored code. If you need an addition service to compliment your app, like a REST API, we got you covered. If you would like us to audit your code base, find potential bugs or performance bottlenecks, we're happy to take a look.

Our Skills

The ones that pay the bills

Development

Our people are multilingual problem solvers. here are some of the technologies we're proficient in: Ruby On Rails, Test Driven Development, Agile methodologies, REST, API's, PostgreSQL, Redis, Web-Scraping, Oauth, Heroku, Digital Ocean, Amazon Web Services, JavaScript, Node, Express, WebSockets, HTML5 Canvas, New Relic, Skylight, Chrome Dev-Tools, WordPress, Static Blog Generators.

Design

Want to hear our dirty little secret? We love design! Users usually only see the front-end of an app/web-site and we want that first impression to be pleasant. At Alkami, we know that usability is a crucial part of a users experience. We take great pride in designing cool simple interfaces. Our experts understand user experience and build intuitive interfaces. We're proficient in SCSS, Bohemian Sketch, Mobile First Design, and UX/UI principles.

Communication

Communication is a key component of any project. Instead of keeping you in the dark, we keep you involved. We view every project as a partnership. By constantly keeping you in the loop, we're able to create a better product and avoid any confusion or misunderstanding. You know exactly where your project stands and where it's going. You always have access to us through chat, email, and telephone.
